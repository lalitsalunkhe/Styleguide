Frequently asked questions

Is eCareLTC HIPAA-compliant? −
Yes. Every product — eMAR, clinical charting, and billing — is built with HIPAA and HITRUST compliance at the core. Resident data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
Does it support both skilled nursing and assisted living? +
Yes. eCareLTC is built for the full post-acute continuum: SNF, ALF, memory care, and group homes.
How long does implementation take? +
Most facilities go live in under two weeks with a dedicated onboarding manager handling data migration and staff training.
Can it handle Medicaid and Medicare billing? +
Yes — claims for Medicaid, Medicare, and private pay are automated, with census and payer-mix tracking built in.
