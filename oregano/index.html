FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Is the platform HIPAA-compliant? −
Yes. Every product, including the EHR, billing software, and AI features, is built with HIPAA compliance at the core. Patient data is stored securely, handled according to strict privacy protocols, and protected through encrypted workflows.
How does the platform help private practices grow? +
Through connected marketing, scheduling, and reputation tools that bring new patients in and keep them engaged.
What makes this platform different? +
One platform unifying EHR, billing, patient experience, and growth — instead of stitching point solutions together.
Is it easy to switch from another system? +
A dedicated onboarding manager handles data migration and training so most teams go live in days.
What types of specialties are supported? +
Primary care, mental health, and dozens of other independent specialties.
